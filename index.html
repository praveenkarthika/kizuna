Why we exist?

Kizuna is our way of saying thank you to the community.

We bring healthy and nutritious millet-based foods to your home while supporting farmers and their growth.

Gratitude and Health for the community, Support for farmers.


Deliver healthy and nutritious millet-based food products that improve the health and well-being of our customers.

Promote stable farming by increasing the demand for millets, supporting farmers, and improving their prosperity.

Promote millets as a superfood to inspire healthier eating habits within society

Build a trusted brand known for health, sustainability, and authenticity in millet-based foods.

Make millets the backbone of farming and prosperous communities.

Build a long-lasting food ecosystem that values tradition, health, innovation, and ethical practices.

Health First: We prioritise the well-being of our customers by delivering nutritious millet-based food products.

Community Empowerment: Supporting farmers and encouraging sustainable livelihoods is at the heart of what we do.

Authenticity: We maintain transparency and integrity in our ingredients, processes, and promises.

Innovation: We embrace creativity to develop unique, delicious, and health-focused food solutions.


Kizuna Millet Foods is a millet food manufacturing company. Its products are Millet Cookies, Millet Health Mix etc.,
